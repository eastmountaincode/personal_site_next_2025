HTML Pollinator Garden is like a Little Free Library but for files instead of books. It's a small e-ink device with a QR code that lets anyone share files with each other: images, songs, PDFs, whatever.

There are many HTMLPG devices in the wild, hosted by small businesses and community spaces. All devices share the same 4 "boxes".

Files come and go. When someone downloads a file, it disappears from the box for everyone, just like borrowing a book. The QR code changes every 30 minutes ensuring that files come from people who were physically present at the location of the HTMLPG device.

## Concept 

I wanted to create a place people could go to find something spontaneously, and wonder about the person who put the file there. So much of what I love is in files, so I wanted to bring the experience of a lending library to the digital realm.
